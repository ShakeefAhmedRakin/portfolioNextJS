## About AgriSmart

AgriSmart was our 24-hour project — a logistics management platform for farming operations. It tracked seeds, tools, and machinery with personalized dashboards for resource and stock-level monitoring, plus a Gemini-powered AI Agri-Guide that answered farm-related questions for new farmers and a community forum for knowledge-sharing.

![AgriSmart Project](agrismartcard.png)

## Reflections

This was my first time building and shipping a full-stack application — including AI integration — inside a single 24-hour cycle. The constraint forced fast prioritization and tight team communication: my teammates relayed ideas and I coded them into the platform piece by piece. We left with a working demo and a Top 10 finish.
