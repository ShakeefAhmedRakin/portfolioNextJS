## About the Course

The Complete Web Development Course by Programming Hero is a project-based MERN bootcamp covering MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, modern JavaScript, Redux, and Next.js. The program runs as weekly modules with hands-on assignments, two larger projects, and daily live support, with performance-based access to follow-on tracks like Early Job Placement and SCIC.

## My Experience

This is where my full-stack foundation came from. The combination of weekly project deadlines and live feedback turned MERN from "things I'd read about" into something I could ship. Finishing on time with a high score qualified me for SCIC and the Early Job Placement track, which I joined next.

## What I Learned

- **MERN fundamentals**: Tailwind, React hooks, async JavaScript, Node/Express, MongoDB/Mongoose
- **Project workflow**: breaking down tasks, version control with git, and deploying production apps
- **Interview prep**: structured practice through follow-on coaching that pushed me toward job-readiness
