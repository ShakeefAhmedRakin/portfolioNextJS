## About the Course

SCIC (Super Charged Interviewee Club) is a six-week career-readiness program offered by Programming Hero to top graduates of its main web development course. It covers job-search strategy, LinkedIn and GitHub optimization, portfolio building, and interview practice. The Black Belt is its highest tier, awarded based on assignment scores (85%+) and final interview performance (25–27/30).

## My Experience

This is where I went from "I built things in a course" to "I can talk about what I built in an interview." Mock interviews with feedback, structured practice on the storytelling around projects, and the GitHub/LinkedIn polish made a measurable difference in how I present my work. The Black Belt was the recognition for that.

## What I Learned

- **Job-search strategy**: targeted outreach and networking on LinkedIn and GitHub
- **Self-presentation**: framing projects and skills clearly, both written and live
- **Interview practice**: structured mock interviews and storytelling-around-projects coaching
