Software Developer focused primarily on **backend and desktop application development** using .NET technologies, with additional contributions to modernization efforts involving cross-platform and AI-assisted tooling. Worked on domain-heavy **environmental modeling systems** combining agriculture, economics, and emissions accounting.

##### [HOLOS-IE](https://www.ucd.ie/holos-ie/)

The **Irish-specific evolution** of the Canadian Holos model, leveraging large datasets, machine learning, and GIS to simulate how farming practices affect a farm's total carbon balance. Built with **C# and Telerik UI**.

- Built the **agroforestry economics** module end-to-end, delivering a complete cost–revenue and farm management analysis across multi-period cycles.
- Built the **dairy cattle economics** module from start to finish, modeling herd-level financial flows and operational economics across full management cycles.
- Designed and documented **IPCC Tier 2–based emission factor models**, including **soil organic carbon (SOC)** and **nitrogen pool dynamics** (initiation, decay, and steady-state behavior).
- Conducted **research and data engineering** on large-scale environmental and agricultural datasets, supporting model accuracy and system validation.

##### [HOLOS-EU](https://www.linkedin.com/company/holoseu/)

An **EU-wide evolution** of the Holos model designed to standardize how European farmers and policy-makers assess the environmental footprint of sustainable agricultural production, aligned with the European Green Deal. Built with **C# and Avalonia UI**.

- Developed numerous **front-facing UI components** in Avalonia and wired them up to backend services.
- Prepared **backend domain models** underpinning the emissions and farm management workflows.
- Contributed to the **HOLOS-EU modernization initiative**, building a cross-platform demo using **Electron, React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, FastAPI**, and AI-assisted analytics to improve usability and scalability.
