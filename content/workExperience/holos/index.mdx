##### [HOLOS-IE / HOLOS-EU](https://www.ucd.ie/holos-ie/)

HOLOS-IE is a data-driven modeling tool that supports sustainable land-use management and carbon accounting across agricultural systems.

- Developed and maintained application modules using **C#**, **MVVM (Avalonia)**, and **Telerik UI** for precision agriculture workflows.
- Implemented **Agroforestry** and **Dairy Cattle Economics** modules featuring cost–revenue modeling across management periods.
- Designed and documented **IPCC Tier 2–based carbon emission factor algorithms** for **Soil Organic Carbon (SOC)** and **nitrogen pools**, including initialization, decay rate, and steady-state computations.
- Conducted **research and workflow documentation** for agricultural modeling and large-scale environmental data management.
- Contributed to the **HOLOS-EU UI modernization initiative**, developing a **cross-platform demo** using **Electron, React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, and FastAPI** to improve usability and portability.
