##### [HOLOS-IE / HOLOS-EU](https://www.ucd.ie/holos-ie/)

HOLOS-IE is a data-driven modeling tool that supports sustainable land-use management and carbon accounting across agricultural systems.

- Develop and maintain application modules using **C#**, **MVVM (Avalonia)**, and **Telerik UI**.
- Implement **Agroforestry** and **Dairy Cattle Economics** modules, including cost–revenue modeling across management periods.
- Design and document **IPCC Tier 2–based carbon emission factor** calculations for **Soil Organic Carbon (SOC)** and **nitrogen pools**, covering initialization, decay rates, and steady-state computations.
- Conduct research and prepare workflow documentation for agricultural modeling and data management.
- Contribute to the **HOLOS-EU UI modernization proposal** and develop a **cross-platform demo** using **Electron**, **React**, **TypeScript**, **TailwindCSS**, and **FastAPI**.
